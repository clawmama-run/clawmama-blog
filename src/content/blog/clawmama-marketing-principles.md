## What makes the ClawMama voice work

ClawMama’s external voice should stay consistent: clear, practical, warm, and a little playful. We avoid hype and focus on outcomes.

### Pillars

- **Honest value first**: Every campaign explains what the audience gets right up front.
- **Simple actions**: Use short steps and concrete examples.
- **Consistency over volume**: Favor a steady publishing rhythm.

## Copy and visuals

Keep layouts clean and leave enough white space. Use restrained colors, generous spacing, and human moments.

### First draft checklist

- Is the headline specific?
- Can the first paragraph be understood in 10 seconds?
- Does the post link to one clear action?
